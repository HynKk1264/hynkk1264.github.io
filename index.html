of Ben Tre's Premier Tourist Destination

Lan Vuong Tourism Area is currently the most popular destination for sightseeing, entertainment, and fun in Ben Tre. Built in a distinctive Western style, Lan Vuong is renowned for its beautiful natural landscapes of rivers and water, exquisite local cuisine, and a wide range of exciting experiential activities. Moreover, it is also a paradise for cuisine-building trips, tourism, and weekend getaways.
